Need Finding Activity

Problems:

1. There are professors that lack the initiative to inform students that they will not be present.

2. Student waste their time waiting for their professors who turn out to be absent.

3. There are available resources that the professors do not utilize to communicate with their students.

4. Some students are shy to ask the lady in the reception to know if their professors are present for that day.

5. The students don't know if their professors are present or not.

6. Instead of doing something necessary, students tend to wait for their professor who aren't present on that day.

7. The money rendered by the student' parents are sometimes wasted because some professors do not come in class without notifying the students.

Solution:

1. Something that will notify the students about their professors' attendance.

2. An application that will automatically communicate the students.

3. An application that does not require internet connection.

4. Something that will solve specific problem that only a typical student will understand.

5. Something that will value the money of the paying parents of students.
